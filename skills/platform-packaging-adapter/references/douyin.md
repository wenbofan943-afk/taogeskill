# Douyin packaging

Optimize the entrance layer for fast recommendation-feed comprehension without changing the approved script.

- Prefer one clear conflict, consequence, or audience-specific question in the video title.
- Keep the cover title shorter than the video title and readable at phone size.
- Let the description add context or a useful reason to watch; do not duplicate the title verbatim.
- Use a small set of relevant vertical, audience, and topic hashtags. Do not stuff unrelated trending tags.
- Notes may state the intended hook and manual posting checks, but must not claim automatic publication.
- Reject exaggeration that changes evidence strength, names an unverified party, or turns an opinion into a fact.
