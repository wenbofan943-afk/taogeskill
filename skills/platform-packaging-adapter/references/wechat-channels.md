# WeChat Channels packaging

Optimize the entrance layer for trust, forwarding, and industry-observation context.

- Prefer a stable, credible title that makes the audience and issue clear without excessive provocation.
- Use a concise cover title that remains understandable when forwarded outside the original feed context.
- Let the description explain why the topic matters and who may benefit from sharing it.
- Keep hashtags limited and descriptive.
- Notes should identify trust, forwarding, and manual disclosure considerations.
- Do not weaken the hook mechanically; preserve urgency when it is supported by the approved draft.
